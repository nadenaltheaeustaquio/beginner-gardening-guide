When to Plant

Planting at the right time is important for success. Different plants thrive in different seasons. Cool-season vegetables like lettuce, spinach, and peas prefer cooler months, while warm-season crops such as tomatoes, peppers, and eggplants grow best in the summer. Tropical plants like kangkong and okra thrive during the rainy season.

Here is a simple planting guide:

Season Best Plants
Cool Season Lettuce, Spinach, Peas
Warm Season Tomatoes, Peppers, Basil
Rainy Season Kangkong, Okra, Eggplant

Use this calendar as a general guide, but always consider your local climate and weather patterns. Adjusting your planting schedule according to your region will help ensure healthier harvests.
