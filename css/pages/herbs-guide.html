Easy Herbs for Beginners

Herbs are among the easiest plants to grow, making them perfect for beginners. They take up little space, can be grown indoors or outdoors, and provide fresh flavors for cooking. Basil, mint, and parsley are some of the best starter herbs for new gardeners.

Basil loves sunlight and regular watering. It is often used in pasta, salads, and soups. Mint, on the other hand, grows quickly and spreads fast. To keep it under control, it is best planted in its own pot. Parsley is hardy and can thrive in both sun and partial shade, making it a flexible herb for any garden.

Regular harvesting helps herbs grow bushier and prevents them from flowering too early. Simply cut the top leaves when needed, but avoid removing more than one-third of the plant at once. This ensures continuous growth for months to come.
