Beginner-Friendly Vegetables

Growing vegetables at home is rewarding because you get to enjoy fresh, healthy food directly from your garden. For beginners, it is best to start with easy-to-grow vegetables such as lettuce, tomatoes, and kangkong.

Lettuce grows quickly and can be harvested leaf by leaf. Tomatoes require more care, but they produce plenty of fruit when given enough sunlight and support. Kangkong, or water spinach, thrives in wet conditions and is a popular vegetable in many Asian dishes. These plants give beginners fast results and the motivation to keep going.

Remember to space vegetables properly to avoid overcrowding. Consistent watering and good soil preparation will ensure healthy growth. With just a little effort, you can enjoy delicious and fresh produce from your own garden.
