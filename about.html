About Us

The Beginner Gardening Guide was created to provide simple, step-by-step information for individuals who want to start gardening but feel overwhelmed.

Our mission is to inspire and support beginners by sharing practical knowledge on soil preparation, plant selection, watering, pest control, and seasonal gardening.

Whether you have a backyard, balcony, or just a few pots, this guide is designed to help you create a healthy and thriving garden space.
