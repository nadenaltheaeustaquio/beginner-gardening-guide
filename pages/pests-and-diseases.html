Prevention & Treatment

Use natural remedies like neem oil, soap spray, and introducing beneficial insects (ladybugs, lacewings) to control pests.

Maintain healthy plants through proper watering, fertilizing, and pruning to reduce disease risk.

Remove and dispose of infected plant parts to prevent disease spread.

Rotate crops annually to minimize soil-borne diseases.

For more detailed information, visit The Old Farmer's Almanac Pest Control Guide.
