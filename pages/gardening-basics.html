Introduction to Gardening

Introduction

Gardening is a rewarding hobby that helps you connect with nature. The basics of gardening include preparing healthy soil, choosing the right plants, and providing proper care. By understanding these fundamentals, you can create a thriving garden even as a beginner.

Key Steps

  1. Choose a sunny location for your garden.
  2. Prepare the soil by removing weeds and adding compost.
  3. Select beginner-friendly plants like tomatoes, lettuce, or basil.
  4. Water regularly and monitor soil moisture.
  5. Maintain your plants by pruning and checking for pests.
