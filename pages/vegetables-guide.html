Popular Vegetables to Grow

Tomatoes

Plant in full sun and well-drained soil for juicy fruits.

 Lettuce

Grow in cool weather with consistent watering for crisp leaves.

 Carrots

Plant in loose, sandy soil for straight and healthy roots.

 Peppers

Require warm soil and plenty of sunlight for colorful peppers.
