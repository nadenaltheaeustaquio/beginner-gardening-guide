Soil Preparation

Healthy soil is the foundation of every successful garden. Good soil should be rich, loose, and well-drained.

Fertilizer Basics

  • Organic compost improves soil structure.
  • Use nitrogen-rich fertilizer for leafy plants.
  • Phosphorus helps roots and flowers grow strong.
  • Potassium boosts fruit and vegetable production.
