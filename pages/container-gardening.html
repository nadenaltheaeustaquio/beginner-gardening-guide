Why Container Gardening?

Container gardening is perfect for people with limited space. Whether you live in a small apartment, have a balcony, or just want to decorate your patio, you can still grow healthy plants. It’s also great for beginners since it requires less soil and can be easily managed. With the right containers, soil, and care, you can grow vegetables, herbs, and even small fruits.

Tips

  • Use large pots with proper drainage holes to avoid root rot.
  • Pick lightweight soil mixes to make moving pots easier.
  • Choose compact plants like herbs, peppers, lettuce, and cherry tomatoes.
  • Place containers where they get at least 5–6 hours of sunlight daily.
  • Water regularly, but don’t overwater. Containers dry out faster than garden beds.
