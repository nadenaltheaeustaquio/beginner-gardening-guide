Why Container Gardening?

Perfect for small spaces like balconies, patios, and indoor gardens.

Tips

  • Use large pots with drainage holes.
  • Pick lightweight soil mixes.
  • Choose compact plants like herbs, peppers, and cherry tomatoes.
